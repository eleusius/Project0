Finally, the Eastern religions, especially Hinduism and Buddhism, tend to divide the Universe into a three-fold structure such as body, mind, and spirit. Or alternatively, physical body, subtle body, and causal body; or body, speech, and mind or nirmanakaya, sambhogakaya, and dharmakaya in Buddhism. It is this perennial philosophical story which helped inform my insanity in trying to apply this to physical theory.

For example, the OM in Hinduism is said to be composed of three syllables - A, the origin; OH, the middle; and HUM, the end; plus the silence before and after, which corresponds to the singularity which is the Big Bang and the Black Hole, and the Energy of the Vacuum which allows for virtual particles to be created and destroyed. This “Three Plus One,” when I learned about it, seemed to ring true. And though it may be that the fact that the brain most easily remembers things in groups of three or four, and this is the reason early human beings formed their religions such; it is also possible that the fact that the Universe is structured thus is the reason our brains operate suchly.
